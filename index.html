ICS "Micrologic Design Automation" SRL was set up in 2007 as a branch of SC "Silicon Service" SRL from Romania, in partnership with Micrologic Design Automation Inc. from USA. Our initial mission was to develop semiconductor design software for electronic design automation (EDA) and intellectual property (IP). It enabled integrated circuit corporations to meet their shorten time-to-market objectives, improve nanometer designs quality, reliability, performance and handling multimillion-gate designs. Starting with 2011, we extended our competences in the fields of:

- Digital Design & Verification
- Embedded systems Development
- Automotive

Smart Home appliances

Intelligent Fridge system is designed for a refrigerator which is programmed to sense what kinds of products are being stored inside it and keeps a track of the stock through barcode or RFID scanning. The refrigerator is equipped to determine itself whenever a food item needs to be replenished. The project is a fridge controller with a touch sensing slider bar and touch buttons instead of mechanical buttons. Power consumption should be minimized as much as possible in order to power sensing, user interface and processing side by a peltier element. The temperature difference for peltier operation should be generated by the deep freezer. So line voltage should be applied only to the compressor through a latching relay.
